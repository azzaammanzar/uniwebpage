THE FAMOUS TOYOTA

The Toyota GR Supra is a modern legend, blending Japanese engineering with German precision to create a sports car that’s as fast as it is fun. Under the hood, the 3.0L turbocharged inline-six. With its perfect 50/50 weight distribution, rear-wheel drive, and adaptive suspension, the Supra delivers razor-sharp handling and an engaging driving experience If you’re looking for JDM heritage with modern performance, the GR Supra is calling your name.

The Toyota GR86 is the ultimate driver’s car, delivering pure, lightweight, rear-wheel-drive fun. With its 2.4L naturally aspirated flat-four, pushing 228 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. Weighing just over 2,800 lbs, this coupe is built for precision and agility, making every corner an event. If you want a car that’s affordable, fun, and built for pure driving pleasure, the Toyota GR86 is the one.

The Toyota Land Cruiser is the undisputed king of reliability and off-road dominance. With a rich legacy spanning over 70 years. If you want an SUV that’s built to last, built to explore, and built for you, the Toyota Land Cruiser is your perfect companion.
